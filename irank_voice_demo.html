These are sample questions demonstrating voice input. Tap the 🎙 button on any question, speak your answer clearly, and the transcript appears automatically. You can then edit it or re-record.

How it works:
1. Tap 🎙 — browser asks for microphone permission once.
2. Speak your answer naturally. The transcript appears in real-time (greyed italic) as you speak.
3. When you stop speaking, the final transcript is committed to the answer box.
4. Edit any text you want to correct, or tap 🎙 again to append more.
5. Thrivoy grades the transcript exactly as it would grade typed text.
